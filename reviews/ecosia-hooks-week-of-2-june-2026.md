**Method / caveats**
- The on-screen hook is read from frame **OCR**, not the model's audio transcript. Several clips
  are silent (music only) and the model's narrative misreads them, so OCR is the source of truth.
- "Tone — read from frame" means the automated tone pass was unreliable (silent clip or a
  timeout) and the judgment came from the cover frame.
- Captions are the creator's own per most briefs and can't be checked from the video.
- "Can't verify trending" means we have no audio fingerprinting, so a sound being *currently*
  trending is the creator's to confirm at post time.
